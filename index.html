Plot

After destroying the Death Star, the rebel alliance flees to the ice planet, Hoth. They are pursued by Darth Vader. Later, Luke Skywalker goes to the remote planet of Dagobah receive training from the Jedi Master Yoda. Meanwhile, in Cloud City, Han and Leia and company are captured by the Darth Vader. Luke ends his training early in order to save them. He then confronts Darth Vader who reveals that he is Luke's father.
